Introduction

The Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) Department is dedicated to the study and advancement of electronic systems, communication technologies, and signal processing. Our department offers comprehensive undergraduate and graduate programs that prepare students for a wide range of exciting careers in the rapidly evolving field of electronics and communication.

Programs and Degrees

ECE department offers a range of programs and degrees designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the field of electronics and communication engineering. These include:Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Electronics and Communication Engineering,Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Electronics and Communication Engineering, Ph.D. in Electronics and Communication Engineering, Various specialized certificate programs

Career Opportunities

Graduates from our ECE Department are well-prepared for a wide range of career opportunities in various sectors, including telecommunications, semiconductor industry, research and development organizations, government agencies, and consulting firms. The skills and knowledge acquired during the program equip students to work on cutting-edge technologies and contribute to the advancement of society through innovation and problem-solving.
