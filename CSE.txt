  <h2>Introduction</h2>
  <p>
The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Department is a vibrant and dynamic academic unit dedicated to the study and advancement of computer science and engineering disciplines. Our department offers comprehensive undergraduate and graduate programs, fostering excellence in education, research, and innovation.
  </p>
  
  <h2>Programs and Degrees</h2>
  <p>
CSE department offers a range of programs and degrees designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the rapidly evolving field of computer science and engineering. These include:
        Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering,
        Master of Science in Computer Science,
        Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering,
        Various specialized certificate programs</p>
  <h2>Career Opportunities</h2>
  <p>
    A degree in computer science and engineering opens up a wide range of exciting career opportunities in various sectors, including technology companies, research organizations, government agencies, consulting firms, and entrepreneurship. Our department is committed to equipping students with the necessary skills, industry exposure, and professional development opportunities to thrive in their chosen careers.
  </p>
